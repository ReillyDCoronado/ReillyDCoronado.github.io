3D Model Files

It is relatively easy to find 3D models on the internet, here are some examples of websites that have 3D models, as well as some that let you upload your own.



NASA

NASA doesn't have a wide range of 3D models, but the ones they do have are almost all officially created by NASA and are of a very high quality

Explore

Thingiverse

Thingiverse is a website made specifically to host 3D models and files. The majority, if not all, of the models on the site are created specifically for 3D printing

Explore

Sketchfab

Like Thinigverse, Sketchfab is made to host Models and Files. Some files include animations for the models. However, some models on Sketchfab cost money to download and use.

Explore

There are hunderends of other sites and resources out there that have files for 3D models of course, its not just these. I highly reccomend you go out and look for more!
