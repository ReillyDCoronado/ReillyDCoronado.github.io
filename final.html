3D Models and 3D Printing


I'm sure you've heard about 3D models, and 3D printing before. If you somehow haven't? Well... 3D models are virtual objects, they're made up of things called edges, vertices, and polygons. Each vertex exists within virtual space in a specific spot, the edges connect these vertices with straight lines, polygons are the shapes(usually if not exclusively triangles) formed by the edges.

❮
❯

3D printing is constructing these 3D models (usually ones made specifically for 3D printing) into real life objects with the power of specialized machines, 3D printing usually uses plastic call filament to construct models, but some machines use things like resin or powders.
